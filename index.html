About

S&I INTERNATIONAL MANAGEMENT, INC. is newly established company and 100% Filipino owned company based in Manila City, Philippines. The company is registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and is now licensed by the Philippine Overseas Employment Administration (POEA) to recruit process and deploy land-based Filipino Overseas Worker (OFW). The company was organized by a group of highly qualified professionals working on various fields in the United States of America, Canada, and Middle East countries. Right after the issuance of its recruitment license, S&I International Management, Inc. Intends to become a member of the Association of Recruitment Practitioners of the Philippines Inc. (ARPPI), and the Philippines Association of Service Exporters Inc. (PASEI), two of the most reputed organizations in the recruitment industry.

Vision Statement

  • To be one of the best providers of exceptionally trained skilled-workers and excellent Professionals for both employers and job-seekers with highest form of quality staffing in the global labor market.

Mission Statement

  • Provides the highest caliber employment services to our customers; job seekers and employers;
  • Give flexibility in recruitment options for employers and jobseekers b offering a range of permanent and temporary world force solutions;
  • Provide job seekers access to a coordinated, flexible range of services informed by labor market demand and tailored to their individual needs;
  • Continually evolve our employment services to best respond to our customers need through adherence to a Best Practice and Continuous Improvement management model.

Values

  • Integrity, competency and efficiency are the comerstone of our service delivery principles. Our staffs have a professional approach and treat clients and colleagues with dignity and respect. Implementing these values and practices ensure we are leaders in the employment and recruitment services industry.

What makes us different

Our medical, financial, administrative, marketing and technical proficiencies combined with the wealth of experience in working overseas, our familiarity with the working conditions abroad and our thorough understanding of overseas employment are our best assurance to fine, assess and select the qualified manpower requires by the foreign Principals.

Business Goals and Objectives

Our goal is to provide quality staffing for prospect employers worldwide. To have innovative recruitment and marketing strategies that will be developed to attract applicants to employment, such as targeted recruitment efforts to underutilized labor pools. Our objectives are to brainstorm new recruitment strategies with identified group from outside the country. To meet with groups identified with underutilized employee groups to solicit ideas and build new relationships.

The Selection Process

  • We take sample time to fully understand our client’s business activity and particular needs, the way they operate and type of people they believe will fit in their organizations.
  • Only candidate with suitable qualifications, right experience and attitude to attracts only specific respondents. We also subscribe to leading websites that pool job applicants and analysis of each candidate are given to our clients.

The Cosumentation Process

  • Every job order accepted by the company is treated with care. Principals are advised of all documentary requirements before the actual processing commence. Liaison Officers are assigned to specific activities to assure smooth transition and trace-ability of documents while on process.
  • Documentation, which is vital to development, is handled by a document Specialist to assure that all formalities are done and and completed thus avoiding unnecessary delays.

Medical

  • The Company as a policy deals only with reputable and fully accredited Medical Clinics, guarantees that all workers undergo the rigid medical examination as prescribed and should be physically and mentally fit to work. If in case our worker is already been deployed on the job site and found out that the worker is unfit to work due to medical reasons, S&I International Management, Inc. will replaced the worker at no cost to our clients provided there are valid proofs being presented.

Deployment

  • S&I International Management, Inc. deploys workers within the agreed time frame, normally within 30 days from the date of receiving the recruitment documents and selection confirmation of the candidates. However, delays caused by selected workers undergoing medication or incomplete documentary requirements are not covered by the prescribed deployment period. We see to it that departing workers are in the airport on time while flight details are well coordinated with and communicated to their respective Principals.

Recruitment fees and charges

  • Flexible payment terms are made available to principals to fit their specific requirements, thus making the recruitment process easy and unhampered by prohibitive fees. Placement and Services Fees are dependent on the prevailing rates which are acceptable and workable for all partied concerned. We have readily available options and scheme to fit every Principals policy without compromising.
